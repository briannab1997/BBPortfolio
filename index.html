About Me

I’m Brianna — a Software Engineering student and QA Analyst passionate about creating clean, intuitive, dependable digital experiences. I love building things that feel thoughtful and polished — and breaking things just enough to make them stronger.

I bring hands-on experience in manual QA, UI/UX evaluation, exploratory testing, bug reporting, SQL validation, and API testing with Postman. On the engineering side, I work with Java, Python, JavaScript, C#, and AWS fundamentals, blending creative problem-solving with strong technical depth.

Before transitioning into tech, I spent six years in healthcare as a certified pharmacy technician — sharpening documentation precision, attention to detail, pattern recognition, and calm problem-solving under pressure.

I’m growing toward roles in QA, IT, and software engineering, with long-term goals in automation, cloud engineering, and building digital experiences that feel meaningful and impactful.
